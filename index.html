honeypotflixplay background

honeypotflixplay app is like any movie apps out there where the users/subscribers can watch the movies. I made this app super user-friendly and as descriptive as it can be. The app doesn't consist of too many buttons and links.

The search area is also made simple and obvious to avoid frustration. The movie cards are also made in such a way that users/subscribers can click on it, see a short description before they decide to see the entire description.
